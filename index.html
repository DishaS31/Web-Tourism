Famous Place's To Visit in Amarkantak

Amarkantak is a captivating town offering a rich tapestry of experiences. Discover lush landscapes and serene waterfalls.

Narmada Temple

The Narmada Temple, located at the source of the sacred Narmada River, features a holy kund and several small temples, each significant in its own right. Visitors are drawn to its spiritual atmosphere and historical significance.

Mount And Views

Enjoy the breathtaking views of the Son Mooda mountains in Amarkantak. Surrounded by lush forests, this area is home to the stunning Kapildhara Waterfall, offering a serene escape for nature enthusiasts.

Ancient Temple

Discover the Ancient Hindu Temple at Amarkantak, Chhattisgarh. Known for its architectural excellence, these temples were built by Kalachuri Maharaja Karnadeva and are a testament to the region's rich history.
